Available Positions

We are looking for highly motivated postdoctoral, graduate, and undergraduate research assistants. Applicants with a background in chemistry, physics, biology, engineering, or computer science are all welcome to apply!

For postdocs:
Please send your CV, research statement (3 pages maximum), and the names, emails, and phone numbers of three references to Dr. Zhou (ruobo.zhou@psu.edu).

For graduate students:
Please send your CV to Dr. Zhou (ruobo.zhou@psu.edu) and apply to at least one of the graduate programs:

  • The graduate program in the department of chemistry;
  • The graduate program in the department of biomedical engineering (BME);
  • The Molecular, Cellular, and Integrative Biosciences (MCIB) graduate program at the Huck Institutes of Life Sciences.;
  • Integrative and Biomedical Physiology (IBP) graduate program at the Huck Institutes of Life Sciences.

For undergraduate students:
please send your CV to Dr. Zhou (ruobo.zhou@psu.edu).
